    Step by Step Coping Guides

    Managing Anxiety

    1. Recognise your what triggers you (write them down)
    2. Use grounding techniques (e.g., 5,4,3,2,1 sensory method)
    3. Practice slow breathing for 4-6 minutes
    4. Reach out and text 'YM' to 85258 or call a helpline
    5. Reflect in a journal afterwards

    Coping with Low Mood

    1. Get out of bed and open a window
    2. Drink a glass of water and eat something nourishing
    3. Do one small task (e.g., brush your hair or tidy a corner)
    4. Go outside for 10 minutes
    5. Solo trips

    3. Feeling Overwhelmed

    1. Pause and take 3 deep breaths
    2. Write down everything on your mind
    3. Circle the one thing you can do now
    4. Do it slowly and gently
    5. Reward yourself with something comforting
