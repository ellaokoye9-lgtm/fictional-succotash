Mental health

Mental health is how we feel , think and cope with life.Its not just about being confident and delightful
all the time, but its actually how we deal witht hings like stress, family, school and even making it to the end of the day. Mental health is just like everyone who has physical health, it can go up and it can go down depending on whats going on in our lives.A lot of the times we feel fine, and on the other hand we my feel angry, anxious etc. This is normal as we are all human.A plethora of individuals, especially youg people , struggle with things like anxiety, peer pressure, lonliness and low self esteem.It is extremely hard to talk about especially when no one around may help you.Multiple people feel the same way even if they dont show it . There are places like Mind, Youngmind, Childline and the NHS that offer a range of support and even advice if needed. Conquering mental health is about learning to cope, finding ways to feel better and not being afraid to reach and ask for Help Things like going for a walk, taking solo trips or exercising increase an individualds well being.Mental health is a journey , dont be afraid to start.
