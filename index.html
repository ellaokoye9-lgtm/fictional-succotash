Mental Health

Hello

My name is Daniella and this website was started in 2025 for my Year 12 Extended Project Qualification (EPQ) in sixth form. To make this website, I had to learn how to code using HTML and CSS, research different ways to support others and resources for mental health support.

A side project I've also been working on has been advocation for Mental health during workshops and projects. I am taking part in the Youth commission police where i go to different places that a low socio economic background and spread awareness. I have also undergone training for paganetry where i had made my passion project about mental health and using that platform given to support those around me.

This website will give you a wide variety of resources to help , ranging from numbers you can call or strategies to improve your mood.

Website Sections:

  • Home: This is where you are right now ; the introductory page to me and the site
  • About: Form to send feedback or enquiries, plus contact details
  • Resources: Numbers and strategies like yoga and baking,recipes, tips , self talk, external links etc.
  • Gallery: Step by step instructions, benefits, Workshops and more

Enjoy exploring the site and let me know what you think of it !!
